About Us

LEVELINK INVESTMENTS, founded in 2014 by R. Savanhu, has grown from a small transportation company to a leading provider of freight and container transport in Zimbabwe.

With a focus on customer service and a commitment to excellence, LEVELINK INVESTMENTS has expanded its fleet of trucks and is now well-positioned to lead the industry.

LEVELINK INVESTMENTS is a trusted name in transportation, providing safe and reliable services across Zimbabwe.
With a passion for providing the best possible customer service, LEVELINK INVESTMENTS is destined to become the leader in the freight and container transport industry
